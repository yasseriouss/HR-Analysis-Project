Why Egyptian Companies Choose ZenHR HR System

Automated Payroll & Compliance with Egypt Labor, Tax, and Social Insurance Laws

ZenHR automates Egypt payroll: Income tax, Martyrs Tax, social insurance, and Labor Law EOSB. Manage lateness, OT, and contracts in a fully compliant, accurate system.

End-of-Service (Gratuity) & Contract Management

Automate Egypt end-of-service benefits (EOSB) with ZenHR. Calculate gratuity based on service years, contract type, and labor law. Secure, traceable records ensure compliant offboarding across all branches.

Employee Self-Service (ESS) & Manager Self-Service (MSS)

ZenHR’s bilingual app lets teams request leaves, expenses, and view payslips on the go. Managers approve requests instantly via mobile, reducing HR workload and speeding up operations.

Time & Attendance With Smart Tracking

ZenHR automates attendance in Egypt via GPS, QR, or biometrics. Data syncs with payroll to ensure accurate payouts and eliminate manual errors for field-based and office teams.

Fully Localized for the Egyptian Market

Automate Egypt payroll with Income Tax, Martyrs Tax, and Social Insurance integration. Support local work weeks and holidays via a bilingual platform for Egyptian teams.

Enterprise-Level Security

Secure your HR data with SOC 2 Type II certified protection. Benefit from full encryption, automated backups, and enterprise-grade security tailored for Egyptian businesses.

Is ZenHR fully localized for Egypt?

Yes, ZenHR is built for Egypt’s Labor Law, Social Insurance, ISTIMARA forms, and Martyrs Tax rules.

Does ZenHR support gratuity calculations?

Yes, ZenHR automates EOSB/gratuity calculations based on service duration, contract type, and Egyptian law.

Does ZenHR apply Martyrs Tax automatically?

Yes, where applicable, ZenHR calculates it automatically.

Does ZenHR automate social insurance?

Yes, including employee/employer contributions and social insurance forms.

Can employees use the ZenHR mobile app for attendance?

Yes, GPS, QR, and fingerprint integrations are supported.

Is ZenHR cloud-based?
